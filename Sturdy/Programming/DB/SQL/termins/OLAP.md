stands for **Online Analytical Processing**.
- It is designed for analyzing large amounts of historical data, often aggregated over time, to help with decision-making.
- Common in systems used for reporting and analytics.

#### **Key Features**:

- **Complex queries**: Processes large datasets for trends, patterns, and summaries.
- **Historical data**: Focuses on analyzing past data rather than real-time updates.
- **De-normalized databases**: Data is stored in fewer tables to simplify complex queries.
- **Examples**: Business intelligence tools, data warehouses.

#### **Example**:

Imagine you're analyzing sales trends:

1. You generate a report showing total sales for the past year.
2. You drill down into sales by region, product, or time period.
3. The data helps you decide where to focus marketing efforts.
